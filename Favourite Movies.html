My Favoutite Movies

Three of my all time favourites

Porco Rosso

Porco Rosso explores themes of freedom, identity, and the human spirit in a profound way. Marco Pagot, turned into a pig and known as Porco Rosso, chooses this form to reject a corrupt society. His famous line, "I'd rather be a pig than a fascist," reflects his disdain for the political turmoil of his time and his quest for true freedom. Despite his appearance, Porco embodies honor, bravery, and empathy. The stunning animation and evocative music made a lasting impression on me. "Porco Rosso" is a reflection on the complexities of identity and freedom that I won't soon forget.

La La Land

La La Land is truly exceptional, blending musical, romance, and drama in a way that feels both nostalgic and fresh. The stunning visuals and unforgettable soundtrack, along with the incredible performances by Emma Stone and Ryan Gosling, create an immersive experience. It masterfully balances a tribute to classic Hollywood musicals with a modern exploration of dreams, love, and sacrifice. The innovative choreography and emotional depth left me captivated.

Sherlock Holmes

Sherlock Holmes A Game of Shadows is a thrilling ride that builds on its predecessor with even more intensity and intrigue. The dynamic between Robert Downey Jr. and Jude Law adds depth to the iconic characters, bringing Sherlock Holmes and Dr. Watson to life in a fresh and exciting way. The clever plot twists, high-stakes action scenes, and intricate puzzles make it a gripping watch from start to finish. The film’s stylish direction and sharp dialogue keep you on the edge of your seat, while the antagonist’s stellar performance adds an extra layer of tension and excitement. Really just a lot of fun and my all-time classic feel-good movie.
